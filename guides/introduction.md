# Introduction

This section contains guides for various individuals using the Agrigate platform. Roles include:

- [Consumers](consumer/introduction.md): 
  - Individuals who are using the platform to purchase local food, and interact with producers
- [Producers](producer/introduction.md): 
  - Individuals who run a farm or other small business, and use the platform to sell their products
- [Operators](operator/introduction.md): 
  - Individuals who are running their own instance of Agrigate and need to manage their installation
